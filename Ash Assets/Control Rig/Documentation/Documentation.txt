To Setup the character with control rig, you need to have "humanoid rig" on the character and an "Animator component" attached to it.

Setup : 
1.Go to Tools and open "Control Rig Builder".
2.Give your Character's Animator component as reference in the "Target Animator" slot of the newly opened "Control Rig Builder" window.
3.just click on "Build Rig" and your character is ready with the control rig.
